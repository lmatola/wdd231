Mozambican gastronomy is a rich fusion of flavors, history and different cultures from Africa, the East and Europe. Mozambique offers typical food with a fusion of cultural values ​​that give a special flavor to a mestizo, curious and delicious cuisine.

Zambezian mucapata is considered a dish with a striking flavor full of cultural marks of a people that over generations has been transmitted to their descendants.

Segilola Crey

I love Xiguinha. This dish emerged in the 19th century, in a context where hunger ravaged Mozambican families, as a way of diversifying cassava preparation and thus improving eating habits. It is a traditional dish, often made at wedding ceremonies, with peanuts being a very important condiment.
